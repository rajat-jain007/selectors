Direct Child Selector

  • Styled using Direct Child Selector

  • item 2
Code:- 
        div > ul > li > p 
            {
            background-color: burlywood;
            color: blue;
            font-style: oblique;
            }
Defination:

The Direct Child Selector is denoted by this symbol: (>).
It matches the element matched by the selector that are direct child of the first elements.
